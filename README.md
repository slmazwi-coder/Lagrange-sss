# Static assets

Put your school images in the folders below.

## Suggested sizes
- Hero images: 1920×1080 or larger (landscape)
- Campus photo: 1600×900 or larger
- Principal photo: square image, at least 800×800
- Achievers: portrait or square, at least 800×800

## Folder structure
- `assets/hero/` hero carousel images
- `assets/about/` campus + principal images
- `assets/achievements/` hall of fame and achiever images
